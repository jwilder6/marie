BuffyLevel Help

Phone placement

Place your phone face up on the van floor with the top of the phone pointing toward the front of the van and the left side toward the driver's door. The app reads tilt (side-to-side) and pitch (front-to-back) from the accelerometer.

Reading the results

  • Green wheel — stays on the ground, no rise needed
  • Yellow wheel — moderate rise required
  • Red wheel — needs the most rise

Drive or back onto blocks/ramps under the yellow and red wheels until all four read green.

Buttons

FreezeLocks the current reading so you can move the phone while keeping the numbers visible. Tap again to resume live updates.
CalibrateZeros out the current tilt and pitch. Use when parked on a surface that isn't perfectly flat relative to the van's axles — park roughly level, then tap Calibrate.
HelpThis page.

Rise display

ExactShows rise to the nearest hundredth of an inch.
½″Rounds up to the nearest half inch — useful if your leveling blocks come in ½″ increments.
1″Rounds up to the nearest whole inch.

Van dimensions

Wheelbase (w) — distance between front and rear axles in inches.
Track width (l) — distance between left and right wheel centres in inches.
Defaults are set to the Winnebago Travato (159″ / 71″).

Tip: Add this page to your iPhone Home Screen for instant offline access. In Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen. The app works with no internet connection after the first load.
Tip: If the bubble is moving but the rise numbers seem backwards, your phone may be rotated 180°. Make sure the top of the phone (where the camera is) points toward the front of the van. Tap Calibrate after correcting orientation.
